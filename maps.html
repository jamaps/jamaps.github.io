I like to make maps. Below are links to a number of my cartography projects, as well as a few other visual experiments (data viz, sketches, notes, "art", etc.). These projects vary quite a bit in terms of scope, scale, process, and purpose. They are somewhat ordered chronologically by creation date. Most are available under Creative Commons licenses, and a few I've uploaded to Wikipedia. I have also spent some of my leisure time contributing to OpenStreetMap. Feel free to contact me at jeff.allen@utoronto.ca for any comments or questions.
