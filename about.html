Hello there. Welcome to my website!

At the moment, this place mostly acts as a visual library for a few of my cartography and photography projects from over the years.

In my day job I lead a hodgepodge of data visualization, mapping, front-end web development, spatial data science, and urban research projects at the School of Cities, University of Toronto. Check out our Maps & Data Visualization page if you're interested in the visual part of this work.

Sometimes my work and I are found elsewhere on the internet. Here's where: GitHub, Mastodon, Bluesky, LinkedIn, OpenStreetMap, Google Scholar

Feel free to contact me at jeff.allen@utoronto.ca if you have any comments or questions about anything you find here or elsewhere, or just to say hi! :)
