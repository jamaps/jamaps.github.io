Hello there.

I'm currently a PhD candidate at the University of Toronto. My work and research generally falls within in the domains of geography, urban planning, transportation and (spatial) data science. Here's a bibliography of completed papers, while more technical projects can be found on my GitHub page. My ongoing research examines long-term changes in cities (e.g. suburbanization, transit investments, gentrification) and how these changes affect daily travel and activity participation.

Outside of my day job, you'll either find me exploring the streets of Toronto (usually on foot or by bike), reading, thinking about urban form, or creating maps.

This website is pretty minimal at the moment, but I do hope to expand it to include some of my other projects sometime soon (e.g. photography, reading lists). Feel free to contact me at jeff.allen@utoronto.ca if you have any comments or questions about anything you find here, or if you just want to say hi!
