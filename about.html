Hello there! and welcome to my website. I am a cartographer and urban data scientist based in Toronto. Currently I work as Lead, Data Visualization at the School of Cities. Previous to this, I completed a PhD in Geography focusing on urban dynamics and transportation.

At the moment, this site acts as a visual library for a few of my mapping and photography projects. Feel free to check out my GitHub profile for some of my more technical projects. I am also on Twitter and LinkedIn (but I don't check either super regularly).

Outside of work, you'll often find me exploring the streets of Toronto (usually by foot or by bike), reading, or creating maps.

Feel free to contact me at jeff.allen@utoronto.ca if you have any comments or questions about anything you find here, or if you just want to say hi!
