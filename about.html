Hello there! and welcome to my website. I am a cartographer and data scientist based in Toronto. At the moment, this site acts as a visual library for a few of my mapping and photography projects.

As of 10/2021, I am working towards a PhD in geography at the University of Toronto. I also occasionally work on freelance and consulting projects as time permits. My work and research generally falls within in the domains of urban planning, geography, and/or transportation.

Check out my GitHub profile for some of my more technical projects, while my sporadically updated Google Scholar or ResearchGate pages list published research papers that I've worked on during my PhD.

Outside of work, you'll often find me exploring the streets of Toronto (usually by foot or by bike), reading, or creating maps.

Feel free to contact me at jeff.allen@utoronto.ca if you have any comments or questions about anything you find here, or if you just want to say hi!
